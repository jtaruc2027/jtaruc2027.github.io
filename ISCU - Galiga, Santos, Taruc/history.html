History

As taught in every history class today, the Philippines, in around 2030, had a massive industrialization, causing the sudden yet beneficial wave of space explorations leading to the creation of our new campuses. Our school as a whole, the International State College of the Universe, stands a top the universe as the leading educational institution. (2027 marks the exact date of the passing of the law allowing for our schools to be created.)


We have 4 campuses as of now. One on earth, specifically for its citizens, one on the moon, with specicialized facilities for studying this celestial body, one floating in space orbiting earth, and the last one is on Mars, where out headquarter is located.


Our school has a long history, with it being under a long line of famous schools with renowned members and teachers. It originated way back in the 1800s, along with a famous secret society for intellectuals. Our school aimed to create geniuses to help society. We have some familiar names under our belt, though their biography or records won't show our names. Some notable scholars:

  • J.S. Bach
  • Sir Isaac Newton
  • Leonhard Euler
  • Ryan Gosling
  • Stephen Hawking
