Extra-curriculars

Outside of student-led clubs, our school offers:



Spacewalking

Unironically, this type of extracurricular activity is also called 'extravehicular activity.' This club can be taken by anyone in Campus 3 (Space Station Campus).

Moonwalking

(Not to be confused with Michael Jackson's dance move, but the student-led dance club "InnerSaya" teaches it)
This club covers everything moon surface related, from walking, piloting a rover, collecting samples, and more.

Rocket Science Elective

We can't really get to our other campuses without rockets can we? The earth campus offers an exclusive course on rocket science to build the nation's and institution's future rocket builders.

Volcanology

Mars (where MC and HQ is), is known for having the solar system's tallest volcano, standing at 22km tall. So of course in this campus, Volcanology is a special course one can take to help further understand the inner workings of the red planet.
