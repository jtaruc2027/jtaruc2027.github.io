English and Literature


Our school prides itself in its writers, with generations of great authors and poets have all studied in our walls. This part of the site aims to showcase one of our famous poem collections, known far and wide as: 'The Chronicles of Love and Loss.' This collection focuses on the feelings felt in a relationship (love) then, hits you with how it feels after (loss). There are voice recordings attached for certain poems.




If text is unreadble, refer below. (Carousel can be used to view intended imagery)

My Love are you really mine?

My dearest, are you truly mine, all mine?
Under the moon, hole of light up above,
Would you see me, dear, in the dark thereof?
Would you find me, a grape in a grapevine?
O’ Moon, have you witnessed a love so fine?
Looking at you, your soul from far above,
My heart feels free, peaceful like a dove,
Flying star to star, feeling love divine.
Under moonlit shadows, your sweet embrace
Feels everlasting even after death,
Love, you showed me what my heart was worth.
As we traverse west, into the vast space,
I declare, with every bit of my breath
My love you are mine, unbounded by Earth.
-Jaedon Taruc

Blue Horizons

Monday, dreadful moon, I found myself on
The street, staring at the everblue sky.
Tuesday, all my friends footsteps taunt my lawn;
“Where is she?”, they clamor, “Will she come by?”
Wednesday, a lawn chair and a heart-shaped box,
Your father has a grudge; just a notion.
Thursday with the view of the stretching docs,
Our distance feels as far as the ocean.
Friday, I see you in your pajamas,
I notice we’ll never see eye-to-eye.
Saturday, your shadow, grandest drama,
Left my vision, then Sunday came by.
And on that day I consoled my mother,
“If there’s another, love them like you did her.”
-Jaedon Taruc

Boulevard of Dreams

In candlelight, our world remains aglow
A tale of two, where money doesn't shine
No matter what, our love remains divine
A road of life where ending is unknown
In life's wild journey, I shall hold you near
On this bumpy life, better buckle tight
We’re two kids hitching down the road of life
Our own show, only for us to premiere
When in need, a beam of light shall fill the scene
Uncertain ground, yet we still build our dream
God, the architect with reasons unseen
A master plan, woven in the scheme
For you my dear, are to become my queen
With us together, let us chase that dream
-Seb Santos

Cold Wind Blows

With all i've done, she grasps not my sacrifice
If only she acted right, i'd be with her forevermore
Half my life wasted for her, yet it never had sufficed
I'll never cry for you, you goddamn whore
Girl no more, will i let your voice be on control
Listen to me, go and pay me this debt you owe
I gave my life to you but my time for you, you just stole
Faithfulness was overdosed but you chose to become a hoe
Always in haste, wanting to be by your side
Ever longing for respect, but it's never been in view
All of this, just untrue, kindness, unknowing of it’s disguise,
After all, It seem that our time has reached its due
Our relationship once I tried to protect, began to disconnect as she left
My feelings, forever kept but never to accept, I laughed as she wept
-Seb Santos

Define Love

In silences grasp, where hearts became allure
Unspoken vows, within our souls, define
Two flames that dance their passion pure
Yet shadows cast, as love and losses twine.
No need for words, as silence weaves its spell
A symphony composed in a hushed refrain
In rhythmic beats, our tales of loss do tell
Each note is a chord of love and lingering pain.
Should I unveil the depths of my
tender bloom beneath everyones toxicity
The love yet pain, a heart could not resonate.
Yet shattered by the winds of a cold trickery.
A profound bond, that silence cannot sever,
In solitude, love blooms and losses tether.

A Love for Disarray

As City air hushed on a summer night's embrace
Swift as lightning a slit train's refrain
It races yet can't outrun my inner pain
Awake it lingers a silent haunting grace
Escape, elusive from the monsters' space
In the brain's expanse, where shadows reign
I'm quiet, they say though not in vain
For in my mind, a demon finds its place
Two in the morning, thoughts play their mind
Uncontrolled memories, a nocturnal display
Silent violence in the deep of night
An anomaly, what's labeled as intertwined
The duet sets the scene of disarray
A bet resolves, revealing a inner light
